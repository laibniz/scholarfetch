# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We commit to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free community.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Focus on technical discussion and evidence.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Abusive or disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or ban contributors for violations.

## Reporting
Report concerns privately to repository maintainers.
